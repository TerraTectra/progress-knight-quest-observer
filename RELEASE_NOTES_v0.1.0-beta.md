# Progress Knight Quest: Observer v0.1.0-beta

First public beta of the Observer fork.

Live build:
https://terratectra.github.io/progress-knight-quest-observer/

## Highlights

- Multiverse unlocks from the Void route.
- Multiverse Points are earned passively in real time and are not affected by Time Warp.
- Ten universes are available, each with stronger distortions, local parameters, passive MP weight, and break requirements.
- Multiverse upgrades now support XP, income, lifespan, Evil, Essence, Dark Matter, MP gain, late-route stability, and Observer Signal.
- Universe break costs now scale with the route but are softened by mastery memory and Fracture Memory.
- Observer is available as the third global layer after Universe X.
- Observer subjects simulate their own Progress Knight runs from the beginning and generate Observer Points.
- First Observer subject is free and always Trash rank.
- Added subject ranks, personalities, character strengthening, milestones, insights, loop memory, and Observer upgrades.

## Stability

- Fixed multiple tab crashes and empty-tab states.
- Fixed early unlock regressions where late jobs/skills could appear too early.
- Fixed Evil visibility issues.
- Fixed button rebuild flicker in Multiverse, Metaverse, and Observer tabs.
- Added save compatibility checks.
- Added full meta-path smoke coverage from Multiverse through Observer.
- Added long idle stress coverage for early, multiverse, and observer states.
- Added a unified release check runner.

## Known beta notes

- Balance is expected to move after real play feedback.
- Late universes and Observer subject pacing are the main areas to watch.
- Visual style intentionally stays close to original Progress Knight Quest.
